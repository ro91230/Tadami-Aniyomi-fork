# Product Definition

## Vision
Tadami aims to be the premier Android application for anime and manga consumption, distinguishing itself through a superior user interface and experience. It builds upon the solid foundation of Aniyomi but pivots towards a "design-first" philosophy. The goal is to provide a modern, immersive, and visually cohesive environment that feels native and polished, catering to enthusiasts who value both functionality and aesthetics.

## Target Audience
- **Anime & Manga Enthusiasts:** Users who actively consume content and desire a dedicated, high-quality application for tracking and viewing.
- **Design-Conscious Users:** Individuals seeking a visually appealing and modern alternative to existing open-source manga/anime apps like Aniyomi or Tachiyomi.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Aurora UI:** A comprehensive visual overhaul that replaces the traditional interface with a cleaner, more modern design language, focusing on immersion and smoothness.
- **Refined UX:** A streamlined user experience with simplified navigation and menus to reduce friction and improve usability.
- **Ecosystem Compatibility:** Retains full support for the extensive library of Aniyomi extensions, ensuring users don't lose access to their favorite content sources.

## Key Features
- **Unified Library:** A single, beautifully designed location for all anime and manga.
- **Aurora Design System:** Implementation of the Aurora theme across all screens (Home, Details, Library, Player/Reader).
- **Extension Support:** Seamless integration with Aniyomi extensions.
- **Tracking Integration:** Sync support for major platforms like MyAnimeList, AniList, Kitsu, and Shikimori.
- **Customization:** Modern theming options including light/dark modes and player/reader specific settings.
